# Econ Research Feedback

Lightweight index for structured economics research critique.

## Use For

- Referee-style comments on economics papers, proposals, or slides.
- Paper-code consistency review.
- Identification, data construction, inference, robustness, and contribution
  critique.

## Reference loading

Do not read the whole repository. Open exactly one starting file:

- Full paper review:
  `.resources/econ-ai/AI-research-feedback/Skills/review-paper.md`
- Light paper review:
  `.resources/econ-ai/AI-research-feedback/Skills/review-paper-light.md`
- Paper-code consistency:
  `.resources/econ-ai/AI-research-feedback/Skills/review-paper-code.md`
- Grant or proposal review:
  `.resources/econ-ai/AI-research-feedback/Skills/review-grant.md`

## Review Discipline

- Separate blockers from optional improvements.
- Tie each criticism to evidence in the text, code, or data pipeline.
- Do not say a claim is established unless the design or citation supports it.
- For empirical work, check identification, data construction, inference, and
  robustness before style.

## Boundaries

- Use `econ-writing` for rewriting prose after the critique is decided.
- Use `causal-inference-resources` when the task is mainly method selection.
- Manuscript reviews are read-only unless editing is explicitly requested.
