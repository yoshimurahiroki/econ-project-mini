# Econ AI Resources

Lightweight index for economics-specific AI tooling and research-assistant
workflows.

## Use For

- Discovering AI tools, datasets, courses, and workflows for economists.
- Comparing economist-oriented research-assistant patterns.
- Planning reproducible AI-assisted research workflows without adding always-on
  services.
